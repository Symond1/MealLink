Hunger in India: A Crisis Amidst Plenty

While India ranks as the world's second-largest food producer:

  • ✓ 14% of population faces chronic food insecurity
  • ✓ 38.4% children under 5 show stunted growth (NFHS-5)
  • ✓ Over 20 crore Indians sleep hungry every night

The paradox deepens when we consider that India wastes food worth ₹1.5 lakh crore annually - enough to feed entire Bihar for a year. Urban weddings alone account for 15-20% of this waste, while rural areas face post-harvest losses due to inadequate storage.
